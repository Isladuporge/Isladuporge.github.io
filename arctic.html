In August 2024, I was part of an aerial survey team surveying narwhal (Monodon monoceros) in Naujaat, Nunavut in the Canadian Arctic, with Fisheries and Oceans Canada. The survey utilized two twin otter aircraft flying at 2,000 feet. The results from this manned aircraft survey will be compared with data from a concurrent satellite survey using World View-3 from Maxar Technologies. Additionally, we conducted an eDNA survey from a boat to complement the aerial observations and understand detection distance using this method. A publication detailing our findings is forthcoming.
